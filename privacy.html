Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Animal Hello World ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Animal Hello World YouTube channel and associated tools including our Pinterest automation application (Animal Hello World Auto Pinner). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from viewers or visitors. Our Pinterest application (Animal Hello World Auto Pinner) is used solely by the channel owner to automatically publish pins linking to our YouTube videos. No user data from viewers is collected, stored, or processed.

2. How We Use the Pinterest API

The Animal Hello World Auto Pinner application uses the Pinterest API exclusively to:

  • Create Pinterest pins that link to our YouTube educational videos
  • Read our own Pinterest boards to manage pin placement
  • Schedule and publish content on our own Pinterest account

The application does not access, collect, or store any data from Pinterest users other than the channel owner's own account.

3. Data Storage

We do not store any personal data from viewers, Pinterest users, or YouTube users. OAuth tokens used to authenticate our Pinterest application are stored securely on Google Cloud Secret Manager and are accessible only to the channel owner.

4. Third-Party Services

Our channel and tools interact with the following third-party platforms:

  • YouTube — for publishing educational videos (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Pinterest — for sharing video content (Pinterest Privacy Policy)
  • Google Cloud Platform — for secure credential storage

5. Children's Privacy

Animal Hello World produces content for children aged 2–8. All YouTube videos are designated as "Made for Kids" in compliance with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

6. Cookies

This website does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. We do not run advertisements on this website.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
