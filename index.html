About the Channel

Animal Hello World is a free educational YouTube channel for children aged 2–8. Every short video features a real animal introducing itself — its name, where it lives, what it eats, and the sound it makes.

We believe every child deserves access to high-quality, calm animal education that fosters a love for nature and the world around them.

Who It's For

  • 👶 Toddlers & Preschoolers — Calm 41-second videos for little attention spans.
  • 🍎 Teachers & Educators — Free, captioned, Made for Kids certified. Perfect for classroom animal units.
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Parents & Families — Ad-free, calm content for morning screen time or travel.

Features

  • 🦁 Realistic animals from every continent
  • 🔊 Authentic animal sounds in every video
  • 📖 Full captions for accessibility & literacy
  • 🌍 Animals from Africa, Asia, Americas, Europe & Oceania
  • ✨ New animal friend published every day
